Heart of the Neighborhood

We give back to
the community.

Garry's isn't just a business — we're a neighbor. Every year we invest in the people who support us.

🦃 Annual Thanksgiving Giveaway

Every year, we give out full Thanksgiving meals — turkeys, apple pies, and everything you need — to anyone in the community who needs it. Because family takes care of family.

🤝 Local Events & Support

We host and participate in neighborhood events throughout the year. Our doors have always been open to the community — long before it was trendy to call yourself community-first.

💬 Staff Who Know Your Name

"They call me by name when I come in," says regular customer Kathryn. That's the Garry's standard — a friendly, family-oriented environment where you're always welcome.

📍 North Tacoma, Through & Through

This store has stood on North Pearl Street for over 40 years through multiple owners. The current team is proud to keep the legacy alive and growing for the neighborhood.
